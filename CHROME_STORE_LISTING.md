## Detailed Description (for store listing)

🛡️ **Protect yourself from malicious websites in real-time**

SecureLink automatically scans every URL you visit and warns you before you land on dangerous websites. Our advanced threat detection identifies phishing attempts, malware distribution sites, and other online threats before they can harm you.

**✨ Key Features:**

• **Automatic Scanning** - Every site you visit is checked instantly
• **Real-Time Warnings** - Get alerted before dangerous pages load
• **Phishing Detection** - Identifies fake login pages and scam sites
• **Malware Protection** - Blocks known malware distribution URLs
• **Privacy Focused** - We don't store your browsing history
• **Lightweight** - Won't slow down your browsing

**🔒 How It Works:**

1. Install the extension
2. Browse normally - SecureLink works in the background
3. Get instant warnings when you encounter a dangerous site
4. Click the extension icon anytime to manually scan a URL

**📊 Subscription Tiers:**

• **Free** - 25 scans per day (account required)
• **Pro ($14.99/mo)** - Unlimited scans + email monitoring
• **Enterprise ($59.99/mo)** - Everything + Attack Surface Monitoring

**🏢 Perfect For:**

• Security-conscious individuals
• Remote workers
• Small businesses
• Organizations protecting their employees

**💡 Why SecureLink?**

With cyber threats increasing every day, you can't afford to click blindly. SecureLink gives you peace of mind by verifying every website before you interact with it. Our database is updated continuously to catch the latest threats.

**🔐 Privacy Commitment:**

We take your privacy seriously. SecureLink only sends URLs to our servers for scanning - we never track your browsing history or collect personal information. Read our full privacy policy at https://securelinkapp.com/privacy
