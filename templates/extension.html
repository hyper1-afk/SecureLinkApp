How to Set It Up

1

Install from the Chrome Web Store

Click Add to Chrome on the store page, then confirm by clicking Add extension in the popup. Chrome will install it in seconds.

2

Pin the extension to your toolbar

Click the puzzle piece icon in the top-right of Chrome, find SecureLink in the list, and click the pin icon next to it. The SecureLink shield will now appear in your toolbar at all times.

3

Browse — you're already protected

No configuration needed. SecureLink immediately starts scanning URLs in the background. If you navigate to a malicious or suspicious site, a warning page will intercept you automatically.

4

Sign in to unlock your dashboard (optional)

Click the SecureLink icon in your toolbar and sign into your SecureLink account. This syncs your scan history to your dashboard and gives you access to manual URL scanning, domain health checks, and Pro features if you're on a paid plan.

Tip: You can manually scan any URL directly from the extension popup — just paste a link into the input field and hit Scan. No need to visit the site first.
