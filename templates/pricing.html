Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel your subscription at any time and continue using the service until the end of your billing period.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) through our secure payment processor, Stripe.
What happens if I exceed my daily scan limit?
You'll receive a notification that you've reached your limit. You can either wait until the next day when your scans reset, or upgrade to a higher plan for more scans.
Do unused scans roll over?
No, scan limits reset daily at midnight UTC. Unused scans do not carry over to the next day.
Is there a free trial for Pro or Enterprise?
Our Free plan lets you try the core features. When you're ready to upgrade, you'll be billed immediately but can cancel within 7 days for a full refund if you're not satisfied.
