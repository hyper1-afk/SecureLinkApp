 Stay Safe Online

                    
SecureLink helps you verify suspicious links before clicking them. Protect yourself from phishing attacks, malware, and online scams.

                    
                    

                        
                        

                            
Instant Link Verification

                            
Paste any URL and get a detailed security analysis in seconds.

                        

                    

                    
                    

                        
                        

                            
Risk Level Assessment

                            
Clear risk ratings from Safe to Dangerous help you make informed decisions.

                        

                    

                

                
                
                

                    
 How to Verify Links

                    
Verifying links is simple and takes just a few seconds:

                    
                    

                        
                        

                            
Step 1: Copy the Link

                            
Right-click the suspicious link and select "Copy Link Address".

                        

                    

                    
                    

                        
                        

                            
Step 2: Paste & Analyze

                            
Paste the URL in the input box and click "Analyze Link".

                        

                    

                    
                    

                        
                        

                            
Step 3: Review Results

                            
Check the risk level and detailed analysis before visiting the site.
